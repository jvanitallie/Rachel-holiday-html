A Pretzel Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups of warm water
  • 1 packet of dry yeast
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 tablespoon of brown sugar
  • 3 3/4 cups of all purpose flour
  • coarse sea salt for sprinkling
  • 1 tablespoon of butter

Instructions

  1. preheat oven to 400
  2. whisk yeast into water and let sit for 1 minute
  3. add salt, brown sugar and butter
  4. slowly mix flour until dough is no longer sticky
  5. cover with a towl and let rise for 10 minutes
  6. roll the dough into a rope and form into a pretzel shape
  7. bake for 12-15 minutes or until golden brown
  8. sprinkle with salt and enjoy
